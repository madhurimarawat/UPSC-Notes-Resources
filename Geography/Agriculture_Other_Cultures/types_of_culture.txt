Types of Culture with Description, Techniques:

Agriculture 🌾
Description: Cultivation of crops and rearing of animals for food and resources.
Example Techniques: Crop rotation, organic farming

Horticulture 🌱
Description: Growing fruits, vegetables, flowers, and ornamental plants.
Example Techniques: Greenhouse farming, drip irrigation

Aquaculture 🐟
Description: Breeding and rearing aquatic organisms in water bodies.
Example Techniques: Fish farming, shrimp culture

Sericulture 🐛
Description: Production of silk by rearing silkworms.
Example Techniques: Mulberry cultivation, cocoon processing

Apiculture 🍯
Description: Raising bees for honey and wax production.
Example Techniques: Beehive farming, pollen trap method

Viticulture 🍇
Description: Cultivation of grapes for wine and food.
Example Techniques: Trellis system, canopy management

Floriculture 🌸
Description: Cultivation of flowering and ornamental plants.
Example Techniques: Tissue culture, hydroponics

Silviculture 🌳
Description: Growing and managing forest trees.
Example Techniques: Afforestation, selective cutting

Pisciculture 🐠
Description: Breeding and rearing fish in controlled environments.
Example Techniques: Pond preparation, composite fish culture

Mushroom Culture 🍄
Description: Growing edible and medicinal mushrooms.
Example Techniques: Tray method, bag method
