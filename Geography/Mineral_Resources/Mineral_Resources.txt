🌟 Metallic vs Non-Metallic Minerals: A Quick Overview 🌟

📚 #Definition
#Metallic_Minerals: 💎 Naturally occurring substances containing metal, mined for economic value.
#Non-Metallic Minerals: 🪨 Substances without metals, used in construction, manufacturing, and more.

🔍 Types of Metallic Minerals
1️⃣ Ferrous Minerals: ⚙️ Iron Ore, Manganese, Chromite (Steel, tools, machinery).

2️⃣ Non-Ferrous Minerals: 🪙 Bauxite, Copper, Gold (Electronics, aerospace, jewelry).

3️⃣ Precious Minerals: 💰 Gold, Silver, Platinum (Jewelry, investment).
🔍 Types of Non-Metallic Minerals
1️⃣ Industrial Minerals: 🏗️ Limestone, Dolomite, Gypsum (Cement, glass, plaster).

2️⃣ Energy Minerals: 🔥 Coal, Lignite (Power generation).

3️⃣ Gemstones: 💍 Diamond, Ruby, Emerald (Jewelry).

🌏 India's Major Metallic Minerals
Iron Ore: Odisha, Jharkhand 🏗️ (Steel).
Bauxite: Odisha, Gujarat 🪙 (Aluminum).
Gold: Karnataka (Jewelry).
Zinc & Lead: Rajasthan ⚡ (Batteries).
🌏 India's Major Non-Metallic Minerals
Limestone: Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan 🏗️ (Cement).
Coal: Jharkhand, Odisha 🔥 (Energy).
Mica: Andhra Pradesh 💡 (Electronics).
Salt: Gujarat 🧂 (Edible & industrial use).
🌱 #Environmental _Concerns
🚜 Land degradation and habitat destruction from mining.
🌍 Sustainable practices are essential to minimize pollution.
🌟 Importance
🟦 Metallic Minerals: 🏭 Power industries, infrastructure, modern technology.
🟦 Non-Metallic Minerals: 🏗️ Drive construction, agriculture, energy, and industry.
