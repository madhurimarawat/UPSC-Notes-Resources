## Middle Eastern Countries

### What is the **Middle East**?

The **Middle East** is a **geographical–political region**, not a continent.
The term was coined by **European (British) strategists in the early 20th century** to describe the area **east of Europe**, **west of South Asia**, and **south of Russia**.

📌 **Most accepted meaning today:**

> The region centered around **Western Asia** and **Egypt**, connecting **Asia, Africa, and Europe**, and holding major historical, cultural, religious, and energy (oil & gas) significance.

---

### ❗ Important Notes

* **Middle East ≠ Arab World**
  (Iran, Israel, Turkey are **not Arab**)
* **Middle East ≠ Islamic World**
  (includes Jewish, Christian, and secular states)
* **Afghanistan & Pakistan** ❌
  → Usually classified as **South Asia**
* **Sudan & Libya** ❌
  → Usually classified as **North Africa**, not core Middle East

---

### 🧭 Why the Middle East is important

* Birthplace of **Judaism, Christianity, and Islam**
* Holds **~50% of world’s proven oil reserves**
* Strategic trade routes (Suez Canal, Strait of Hormuz)
* Major geopolitical influence

## 🌊 Gulf Countries

### Middle East & Gulf Countries

❌ **No**, *Middle East* ≠ *Gulf countries*
✅ **Gulf countries are a PART of the Middle East**

---

## What are **Gulf countries**?

These are countries around the Persian (Arabian) Gulf, officially known as GCC countries.
GCC stands for Gulf Cooperation Council - a regional political and economic alliance formed in 1981 to promote economic cooperation, security, and policy coordination among its member states.

**6 Gulf (GCC) countries**

* 🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia
* 🇦🇪 United Arab Emirates
* 🇶🇦 Qatar
* 🇴🇲 Oman
* 🇧🇭 Bahrain
* 🇰🇼 Kuwait

👉 These are the ones usually called **“Gulf countries”** in exams.

---

## What is the **Middle East** then?

The **Middle East** is a **larger region**, which includes:

* **All Gulf countries**, PLUS
* 🇮🇷 Iran
* 🇮🇶 Iraq
* 🇯🇴 Jordan
* 🇮🇱 Israel
* 🇱🇧 Lebanon
* 🇸🇾 Syria
* 🇾🇪 Yemen
* 🇹🇷 Turkey (partly)
* 🇪🇬 Egypt (often included)

---

## Easy exam line to remember

> **All Gulf countries are in the Middle East, but not all Middle East countries are Gulf countries.**
