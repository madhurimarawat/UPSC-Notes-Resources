## 🌍 Largest Oil Reserves **by Continent** *(OPEC data)*

### 🌎 **North America**

1. **Canada** 🇨🇦 – **~168 billion barrels** *(mostly oil sands)*
2. United States 🇺🇸 – **~69 billion barrels**
3. Mexico 🇲🇽 – **~6 billion barrels**

➡ **Largest in North America:** **Canada**

---

### 🌎 **South America**

1. **Venezuela** 🇻🇪 – **~303 billion barrels**
2. Brazil 🇧🇷 – **~13 billion barrels**
3. Ecuador 🇪🇨 – **~8 billion barrels**

➡ **Largest in South America:** **Venezuela**

---

### 🌍 **Asia**

1. **Saudi Arabia** 🇸🇦 – **~267 billion barrels**
2. **Iran** 🇮🇷 – **~209 billion barrels**
3. **Iraq** 🇮🇶 – **~145 billion barrels**
4. **Kuwait** 🇰🇼 – **~101 billion barrels**

➡ **Largest in Asia:** **Saudi Arabia**

---

### 🌍 **Africa**

1. **Libya** 🇱🇾 – **~48 billion barrels**
2. **Nigeria** 🇳🇬 – **~37 billion barrels**
3. **Algeria** 🇩🇿 – **~12 billion barrels**

➡ **Largest in Africa:** **Libya**

---

### 🌍 **Europe**

1. **Russia** 🇷🇺 – **~80 billion barrels** *(includes European & Asian Russia)*
2. **Norway** 🇳🇴 – **~8 billion barrels**

➡ **Largest in Europe:** **Russia**

---

### 🌏 **Australia / Oceania**

1. **Australia** 🇦🇺 – **~4 billion barrels**

➡ **Largest in Oceania:** **Australia**

### 🔑 **Quick Takeaways (Prelims-Ready)**

* 🌍 **World’s largest proven oil reserves:** **Venezuela**
* 🌏 **Most oil-rich continent:** **Asia**
* 🛢️ **OPEC countries hold ~79% of global oil reserves**
* 🇨🇦 Canada’s high ranking is due to **oil sands**, not conventional crude
* 🇻🇪 Venezuela’s reserves are mainly **extra-heavy crude (Orinoco Belt)**
