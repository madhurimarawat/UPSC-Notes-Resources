## 🧭 **FLOWCHART: Ancient Indian History (🪨 to 🏰)**

```
🌏 Prehistoric India (Before 2600 BCE)
     │
     ├── 🪨 Paleolithic Age (c. 2 million – 10,000 BCE)
     │      → Primitive humans, caves, stone tools, fire
     │
     ├── 🪵 Mesolithic Age (c. 10,000 – 8,000 BCE)
     │      → Microliths, domestication begins
     │
     ├── 🌾 Neolithic Age (c. 8,000 – 4,000 BCE)
     │      → Agriculture, pottery, settled life
     │
     ├── ⚱️ Chalcolithic Age (c. 4,000 – 1,500 BCE)
     │      → Copper & stone tools, early villages
     │
     └── 🏙️ Indus Valley Civilization (Harappan) (c. 2600 – 1900 BCE)
            │
            ├── 📜 Early Vedic Period (c. 1500 – 1000 BCE)
            │      → Rig Veda, pastoral life
            │
            └── 🔔 Later Vedic Period (c. 1000 – 600 BCE)
                   → Kingdoms, rituals, varna system
                   │
                   └── 🛕 Mahajanapadas & Kingdoms (c. 600 – 321 BCE)
                          → 16 states, rise of Magadha, Buddhism & Jainism
                          │
                          ├── 🦁 Mauryan Empire (321 – 185 BCE)
                          │      → Chandragupta, Ashoka, administration
                          │
                          ├── 🌸 Post-Mauryan Kingdoms (185 BCE – 320 CE)
                          │      → Indo-Greeks, Kushanas, Satavahanas, art
                          │
                          ├── ✨ Gupta Empire (Golden Age) (320 – 550 CE)
                          │      → Literature, science, culture peak
                          │
                          └── ⚔️ Post-Gupta / Regional Kingdoms (550 – 1200 CE)
                                 → Harsha, Pallavas, Chalukyas, temple art
                  └── ⚔️ Later Kingdoms & Invasions
```

---

## 🪨 **1️⃣ Paleolithic Age (Old Stone Age)**

**⏳ Time:** 2 million BCE – 10,000 BCE
**Mnemonic:** “P for Primitive People” 🧍‍♂️🪨

🔹 Hunter-gatherers, lived in caves

🔹 Tools made of rough stone

🔹 Used fire 🔥

🔹 Art: Bhimbetka rock paintings 🖌️

**Emoji Summary:** 🪨🔥🍖🖼️

---

## 🪵 **2️⃣ Mesolithic Age (Middle Stone Age)**

**⏳ Time:** 10,000 – 8,000 BCE
**Mnemonic:** “M for Mini tools” 🔪

🔹 Microliths (tiny tools)

🔹 Domestication begins 🐕

🔹 Semi-settled life near rivers 🌊

**Sites:** Bagor (Rajasthan), Adamgarh (MP)
**Emoji Summary:** 🪓🐕🌾

---

## 🌾 **3️⃣ Neolithic Age (New Stone Age)**

**⏳ Time:** 8000 – 4000 BCE
**Mnemonic:** “N for New Life – Farming & Houses” 🏡

🔹 Started agriculture 🌾

🔹 Pottery 🍶

🔹 Domesticated animals 🐄

🔹 Polished stone tools

**Sites:** Mehrgarh (Pakistan), Burzahom (Kashmir)
**Emoji Summary:** 🌾🏡🍶

---

## ⚱️ **4️⃣ Chalcolithic Age (Copper-Stone Age)**

**⏳ Time:** 4000 – 1500 BCE
**Mnemonic:** “C for Copper Charm” 🪙

🔹 Used both copper & stone tools

🔹 Painted pottery 🖌️

🔹 Village life evolved

**Sites:** Malwa, Ahar-Banas, Kayatha
**Emoji Summary:** 🪙🍶🏺

---

## 🏙️ **5️⃣ Indus Valley Civilization (Harappan)**

**⏳ Time:** 2600 – 1900 BCE
**Mnemonic:** “H for High Urban Life” 🏙️

🔹 Planned cities (grid pattern) 🏗️

🔹 Great Bath, Granaries, Drains 🚿

🔹 Trade with Mesopotamia 🚢

🔹 Script undeciphered ✍️

**Major Sites:** Harappa, Mohenjo-Daro, Dholavira, Lothal
**Emoji Summary:** 🏙️🚿🏺🚢

---

## 🔱 **6️⃣ Vedic Age**

**⏳ Time:** 1500 – 600 BCE
**Mnemonic:** “V for Vedas & Villages” 📜

### 🕉️ Early Vedic (1500–1000 BCE)

* Rigveda composed 🕉️
* Tribal life, no large kingdoms

### 🔔 Later Vedic (1000–600 BCE)

* Kingdoms emerge 👑
* Sacrifices, social classes solidified (varna system)

**Emoji Summary:** 📜🕉️🔥👑

---

## 🛕 **7️⃣ Mahajanapadas (600 – 321 BCE)**

**Mnemonic:** “Maha = Great States” 🗺️

🔹 16 major kingdoms — e.g., Magadha, Kosala, Avanti

🔹 Rise of new faiths: **Buddhism & Jainism** 🪷🕊️

🔹 First urban revival after Harappa

**Emoji Summary:** 🏙️🪷🕊️👑

---

## 🦁 **8️⃣ Mauryan Empire (321 – 185 BCE)**

**Mnemonic:** “M = Massive Empire by Maurya” 🦁

🔹 Founded by **Chandragupta Maurya**

🔹 **Ashoka the Great** – spread Buddhism 🌍

🔹 Arthashastra, Edicts on pillars 🪶

**Capital:** Pataliputra
**Emoji Summary:** 🦁📜🌏🪶

---

## 🌸 **9️⃣ Post-Mauryan Kingdoms (185 BCE – 320 CE)**

**Mnemonic:** “Post Maurya = Patchwork Empires” 🧩

🔹 Shungas, Satavahanas, Indo-Greeks, Kushanas

🔹 Gandhara Art, trade & coinage flourish 💰🎨

**Emoji Summary:** 🧩💰🎨

---

## ✨ **🔟 Gupta Empire (320 – 550 CE)**

**Mnemonic:** “G = Golden Gupta Glory” 🌟

🔹 Chandragupta I & Samudragupta

🔹 Classical Sanskrit literature 🌸

🔹 Aryabhata, Kalidasa 🧠📚

🔹 Iron Pillar, Ajanta Caves 🏛️

**Emoji Summary:** 🌟📚🧠🏛️

---

## ⚔️ **1️⃣1️⃣ Post-Gupta & Regional Kingdoms (550–1200 CE)**

**Mnemonic:** “R for Regional Rise” 🏰

🔹 Harsha, Chalukyas, Pallavas, Rashtrakutas

🔹 Temple architecture peaks (Dravida & Nagara) 🛕

🔹 Prelude to Medieval Era

**Emoji Summary:** ⚔️🏰🛕

---
