
UPSC Anthropology Syllabus – Descriptive One-Liners

📘 PAPER I

1. Anthropology 🧠 – The scientific study of humans, their evolution, biology, culture, and societies across time.
2. Relationship with other disciplines 🔗 – Shows how anthropology connects with social sciences, life sciences, medicine, earth sciences, and humanities.
3. Main Branches of Anthropology 🌐 – Includes:
   • Socio-Cultural Anthropology 👥 – Studies human societies, cultures, and traditions.
   • Biological Anthropology 🧬 – Examines human evolution, genetics, and biological diversity.
   • Archaeological Anthropology 🏺 – Explores past human life through material remains.
   • Linguistic Anthropology 🗣️ – Studies language and its role in culture and society.
4. Human Evolution & Emergence of Man 🧑‍🔬 – Explains biological and cultural evolution of humans, evolutionary theories, and key fossil evidence.
5. Primates & Adaptations 🐒 – Covers characteristics, evolutionary trends, and adaptations of primates.
6. Fossil Hominids 🦴 – Details species like Australopithecus, Homo erectus, Neanderthals, and early Homo sapiens.
7. Biological Basis of Life 🧪 – Explains cells, DNA, genes, protein synthesis, mutations, and cell division.
8. Prehistoric Archaeology ⏳ – Principles of dating and cultural evolution from Paleolithic to Iron Age.
9. Nature of Culture 🌍 – Defines culture, its characteristics, ethnocentrism, and cultural relativism.
10. Nature of Society 🏛 – Concepts of society, social institutions, and social stratification.
11. Marriage 💍 – Types, rules, laws, and functions in human societies.
12. Family 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 – Structure, types, and impact of modernization on family systems.
13. Kinship 🔗 – Descent, lineage, kinship terminology, and alliance theory.
14. Economic Organization 💰 – Study of production, distribution, and exchange systems in societies.
15. Political Organization ⚖️ – Forms of political systems and social control in simple societies.
16. Religion & Belief Systems ⛪ – Evolution of religion, magic, myths, rituals, and role in societies.
17. Anthropological Theories 📜 – Key schools like evolutionism, functionalism, structuralism, cultural materialism, post-modernism.
18. Culture & Language 🗣 – Origin of language, communication, and its social context.
19. Research Methods in Anthropology 📊 – Fieldwork, data collection techniques, and analysis methods.
20. Human Genetics 🧬 – Genetic principles, inheritance, chromosomal aberrations, genetic counseling.
21. Race & Racism 🌍 – Biological variation, racial classification, and race crossing.
22. Human Variation & Adaptation ❄️🔥 – Adaptations to environmental stress like heat, cold, and altitude.
23. Epidemiological Anthropology 🏥 – Health, disease patterns, and nutrition-related disorders.
24. Growth & Development 👶➡️👴 – Stages of growth, aging, factors influencing development.
25. Fertility & Demography 👩‍🍼 – Biological events, demographic theories, and fertility patterns.
26. Applications of Anthropology 🛠️ – Forensics, genetics, sports, nutritional anthropology, and DNA technology.

📗 PAPER II

1. Indian Culture & Civilization 🇮🇳 – Evolution from prehistoric to protohistoric periods and contributions of tribal cultures.
2. Palaeo-Anthropology in India 🦴 – Fossil evidence like Ramapithecus and Narmada Man.
3. Ethno-Archaeology 🔍 – Use of living communities to interpret past cultures.
4. Demographic Profile of India 📈 – Ethnic, linguistic diversity, and population structure.
5. Traditional Indian Social System 🕉 – Varnashrama, Purushartha, Karma, and rebirth concepts.
6. Caste System in India 🏷 – Structure, theories of origin, mobility, and future of caste.
7. Sacred & Nature-Man-Spirit Complex 🌿 – Religious and cultural ecological systems in India.
8. Religious Impact on Indian Society ⛩ – Effects of Buddhism, Jainism, Islam, and Christianity.
9. Growth of Anthropology in India 📚 – Contributions of scholars and administrators.
10. Indian Village Life 🌾 – Social structure, caste relations, agrarian changes, and globalization impact.
11. Minorities & Marginalized Groups 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 – Status of linguistic and religious minorities in India.
12. Processes of Social Change 🔄 – Sanskritization, Westernization, modernization, and media influence.
13. Tribal Situation in India 🏹 – Genetic variability, socio-economic characteristics, and distribution.
14. Tribal Problems ❗ – Issues like land alienation, poverty, education, health, and displacement.
15. Development & Tribals 🏗 – Impact of industrialization, forest policy, and rehabilitation.
16. Exploitation of Weaker Sections ⚠️ – Deprivation of SCs, STs, OBCs and constitutional safeguards.
17. Tribal Social Change & Politics 🗳 – Impact of democratic institutions, ethnicity, and regionalism.
18. Role of Religion among Tribes ✝️ – Effects of major religions on tribal societies.
19. Tribe and Nation-State 🌎 – Comparative studies of tribes in India and globally.
20. Tribal Administration & Development 🏛 – History of policies, PTGs, NGOs, and tribal welfare programs.
21. Role of Anthropology in Development 🤝 – Application in rural, tribal development and policy-making.
