### 🧠 **Exam Gold Notes**

* ✔️ **PMO complex officially designated as “Seva Teerth”** (means *pilgrimage of service*)
* 🏛️ **Raj Bhavan → Lok Bhavan** & **Raj Niwas → Lok Niwas** reflect people-centric governance
* 🏗️ **Kartavya Bhavan ≠ Ministry renaming** (only office complexes)
* ⚖️ **BNS / BNSS / Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam = extremely important for SSC, Banking, UPSC**
* 🛣️ **Rajpath → Kartavya Path** = symbolic governance shift

## ⚠️ **Partially controversial / needs careful wording**

### 1️⃣ **PMO → “Seva Teerth”**

* ✔️ **Confirmed:** The **PMO complex/building** has been designated **“Seva Teerth”**
* ❌ **Not correct to say:** “PMO has been renamed”
* ✅ **Correct exam wording:**

  > *PMO complex is officially designated as Seva Teerth; the institution remains the Prime Minister’s Office.*

📌 This distinction **matters a LOT in exams**.

---

### 2️⃣ **Raj Bhavan → Lok Bhavan / Raj Niwas → Lok Niwas**

* ✔️ **Confirmed:** Renaming **has happened in some states / specific contexts**
* ❌ **Not uniform nationwide**
* ❌ **No single central gazette mandating all Raj Bhavans to change**
* ✅ **Correct framing:**

  > *Renamed in certain states/administrations, not a pan-India change.*

📌 This is **administrative, not constitutional**.
