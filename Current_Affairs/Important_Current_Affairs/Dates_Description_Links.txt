12. Union Budget of India 2026-27 — 1 February 2026

The Union Budget of India for the financial year 2026-27 was presented on 1 February 2026 by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman. The budget outlines the government’s fiscal policy, expenditure priorities, and key reforms for the year, emphasizing infrastructure-led growth, manufacturing, and fiscal discipline.

Total expenditure was estimated at about ₹53.5 lakh crore, with non-debt receipts around ₹36.5 lakh crore and a fiscal deficit target of 4.3 % of GDP. Public capital expenditure was increased to around ₹12.2 lakh crore, showcasing a strong push for infrastructure investment.
