1. Deccan Traps – Panchgani & Mahabaleshwar (Maharashtra)
   🟤 Vast volcanic basalt rock formations showcasing Earth’s fiery past.

2. St. Mary’s Island (Karnataka)
   🔵 Unique hexagonal basalt columns surrounded by the Arabian Sea.

3. Meghalayan Age Caves (Meghalaya)
   🟣 Ancient caves formed over millions of years, mysterious and deep.

4. Naga Hill Ophiolite (Nagaland)
   🟢 Geological marvel of rocks from oceanic crust, amidst green hills.

5. Erra Matti Dibbalu (Andhra Pradesh)
   🔴 Famous red sand dunes carved by wind and water over ages.

6. Tirumala Hills (Andhra Pradesh)
   🟠 Sacred hills known for spirituality and scenic landscapes.

7. Varkala Cliffs (Kerala)
   🌸 Stunning coastal cliffs along the Arabian Sea, unique in India.
