# 🧠 **UPSC Civil Services Cheat Sheet** (All Papers)

*Smart Summary for Prelims, Mains (GS I–IV), Essay, Ethics & More*

---

## 📝 **1. Prelims Cheat Sheet**

**👉 Objective Exam | 2 Papers | Qualifying & Screening only**

### 📘 **Paper I: General Studies (200 marks)**

🧾 Subjects:

* 🇮🇳 Polity – Constitution, governance, Panchayats
* 🌍 Geography – Indian + World
* 📜 History – Ancient, Medieval, Modern, Freedom struggle
* 💰 Economy – Basic concepts, Budget, RBI
* 🌱 Environment – Biodiversity, Acts, Reports
* 🧪 Science & Tech – Current developments, Basics
* 🗞️ Current Affairs – Last 12–18 months

✅ Tips:

* Revise **NCERTs (6–12)** 📚
* Focus on **static + current mix** 🔄
* Eliminate options smartly ❌✅

---

### 📗 **Paper II: CSAT (200 marks)**

⏳ Qualifying only (33% or 66 marks)

💡 Areas:

* 🧮 Maths – Class X level (Ratio, Percentage, Geometry)
* 🧠 Reasoning – Logical puzzles, directions, coding
* 📖 Comprehension – Reading passages
* ✍️ Decision-making – Situational judgment

✅ Tips:

* Practice with **previous year papers**
* Focus on **accuracy & time** ⏱️

---

## 📚 **2. Mains Cheat Sheet**

**👉 Written Descriptive | 9 Papers | 1750 marks total**

---

### 🧾 **Essay Paper (250 marks)**

* 2 essays x 125 marks
* Topics: Philosophical, Governance, Economy, Ethics
* Use **multi-dimensional** analysis (social, political, ethical)
* Structure:
  🔹 Introduction
  🔹 Body (with headings)
  🔹 Conclusion (forward-looking)

✅ Include: Real examples, quotes, case studies 🧠
❌ Avoid: One-sided views, jargon, random facts

---

### 📕 **GS Paper I – Indian Heritage & Culture, History, Society (250 marks)**

📜 History:

* Art & Architecture
* Freedom struggle leaders, movements
* Post-independence developments

🌍 Geography:

* Physical (climate, rivers, disasters)
* Economic & human geography
* Maps + locations 🗺️

👥 Society:

* Diversity of India, population, women, urbanization
* Issues: caste, secularism, communalism

✅ Use: Diagrams, maps, and case studies
✅ Source: NCERTs + Nitin Singhania + Geography by G C Leong

---

### 📘 **GS Paper II – Polity, Governance, IR (250 marks)**

📜 Constitution:

* Features, amendments, FRs, DPSPs, federalism
* Judiciary, Parliament, Election Commission

🏛️ Governance:

* RTI, NGOs, SHGs, e-governance
* Welfare schemes, health, education

🌐 International Relations:

* India’s neighbours
* International groupings (BRICS, SCO, QUAD)
* Foreign policy shifts

✅ Use: SC judgments, committee reports, current examples
✅ Source: Laxmikanth + ARC reports + MEA site

---

### 📗 **GS Paper III – Economy, Environment, S\&T, Internal Security (250 marks)**

💰 Economy:

* Budget, Banking, Fiscal policy, Agriculture
* Inclusive growth, Infrastructure, MSMEs

🌱 Environment:

* Acts, EIA, pollution, climate change treaties
* Biodiversity, wildlife conservation

🧪 Science & Tech:

* Space, Biotech, AI, Quantum computing
* Health tech (COVID vaccines, genomics)

🔐 Internal Security:

* Terrorism, cybercrime, Naxalism
* Role of agencies (NIA, IB, NSG)

✅ Use: Data, government schemes, diagrams
✅ Source: Economic Survey, PIB, Down To Earth, Ramesh Singh

---

### 📙 **GS Paper IV – Ethics, Integrity, Aptitude (250 marks)**

🧠 Ethics Theory:

* Thinkers: Gandhi, Aristotle, Kant, Rawls
* Moral philosophy: deontology, utilitarianism
* Values: integrity, compassion, objectivity, empathy

🧰 Applied Ethics:

* Case studies (decision-making, conflict of interest)
* Civil service values
* Attitude, emotional intelligence

📦 Tools:

* Quotes 📜 (“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.” – Gandhi)
* Ethical dilemmas
* Diagrams like stakeholder mapping 🧭

✅ Use: Structure: Intro → Ethical issue → Analysis → Decision → Justification
✅ Source: Lexicon, ARC reports, previous year papers

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ **3. Optional Subject (250 x 2 = 500 marks)**

🎯 Choose based on:

* Interest
* Background
* Availability of material
* Overlap with GS

Popular optionals: PSIR, Anthropology, Sociology, Geography, History

✅ Strategy:

* Master syllabus
* Use toppers’ notes & past papers
* Write answers with thinkers/theories/examples

---

## 🗣️ **4. Personality Test / Interview (275 marks)**

🧑‍💼 What matters:

* Clarity of thought
* Honesty & integrity
* Understanding of DAF (Detailed Application Form)
* Opinion on current issues

💡 Be prepared for:

* Current affairs
* Background questions (education, hobbies)
* Situational questions (ethical, administrative)

✅ Read: Newspaper daily (The Hindu/Indian Express)
✅ Mock interviews with honest feedback

---

## 🧾 **5. Smart Study Tools & Tips**

### 📅 Weekly Routine:

* 3 days for GS
* 2 days for optional
* 1 day for essay, ethics, answer writing
* 1 day for revision + test

### 📌 Must-Use Sources:

* 📚 NCERTs (6–12)
* 📕 Laxmikanth (Polity)
* 🧪 Science – Current affairs-based
* 🌿 Shankar IAS (Environment)
* 📉 Economic Survey + Budget Highlights
* 🗞️ Newspapers + PIB + Yojana/Kurukshetra
* 📑 UPSC previous year question papers

---

## 🔍 Bonus: How to Practice Answer Writing ✍️

* Use **intro-body-conclusion** (IBC) format
* Add headings and subheadings 🔤
* Use data, reports, SC cases, diagrams
* Practice daily (even 1 answer = progress)
* Get feedback from peers/mentors
